Fundraising

With inflation and rising energy costs, fundraising is now more important than ever! This year we need to raise £125,000 in additional funding for the school. We’ve worked closely with the teachers, governors and parents to agree a target that will make a huge difference to the opportunities we provide our children. This funding will go towards on going opportunities beyond the core curriculum such as outdoor learning, book week, school trips, art week, music enrichment, language lessons, our dedicated PE teacher, as well as one off projects like helping to create more enriching and neurodiverse classrooms.

How will we raise this money

Monthly Donations

The ABC fund is set up to enable us to go further in meeting the school ambitions, values and ethos of the school and improve the experience of all the children.

Last year we received £47,000 of which £11,326 was used to pay for building maintenance and the rest was spent on creating wonderful opportunities such as African drumming, violin and trumpet lessons. outdoor learning, school trips, and language lessons.

The ABC fund is a suggested £20 monthly direct debit donation made by parents via the link below. 

Parents who can afford to give more are kindly encouraged to do so. https://cafdonate.cafonline.org/6771#!/DonationDetails#%2FDonationDetails

PSA (Parent Student Association)

The PSA’s mission is to enrich our children’s experiences and enhance their wellbeing. Generous parents give up their time and money to fundraise through sponsored events for the things the school really needs beyond the every day essentials. Most recently the PSA has helped fund the Astroturf, refurbished the school hall, provided new AV equipment and put on events such as Art and book week.Last year the PSA raised £38,000.  Over the next three years the PSA is focused on the redecoration of all our children’s classrooms to create more enriching an neurodiverse classrooms.

If you are interested in getting involved in volunteering or leading an event please contact psa@princessfrederica.com

Download the easifundraising app and type in your cause (Princess Frederica school), and then get shopping, with major brands such as amazon giving back 1.5%! Last year we made £5000 for the school, so it really does add up.

Friends of Freds

Kensal Rise and Kensal Green are characterized by a strong community spirit and many of our local businesses understand that support of local schools has a positive impact on their image. We want to offer opportunities for on-going partnerships between local companies and Princess Frederica’s PSA. I return we will offer the business the ability to categorise their investment as a charitable donation, as well as benefit from the exposure of their business to our parent community.

If you are a business who would like to get involved please contact

psa@princessfrederica.com

Matched Giving

Some employers have schemes that match your personal charitable donations, as the PSA and ABC fund are charities if your business does offer this, then they will be able to match any donations you give
